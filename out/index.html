ABOUT THE PROJECT

We get together to share our experience, views and insights that can help tech startups to easier navigate the deep water of legal issues.

We have extensive experience of advising tech companies on different legal matters occurring during companies lives, and we will be happy if our experience will help you to approach legal questions.

HOW IT WORKS

Step 1

You fill out our

Step 2

We contact you if necessary to clarify the details of your request.

Step 3

You have a mentoring session. Each session lasts one hour. You can have up to 2 sessions per request.

Step 4

We collect your feedback.

This is a pro bono mentorship project, we do not provide legal services such as advising on transactions, drafting documents or representing your company in a court. Before making any decision, you are always advised to get legal advice or consultation.

PRINCIPLES

Love for technology and innovation

We believe that technology is changing the world for the better and opens new horizons for the peoples development and growth, thats why we love it and want to support companies driving positive changes through the technology-based solutions.

Honesty and open dialog

We know that sometimes our expertise, background or other circumstances make it difficult or impossible for us to deal with certain matter. We will always tell you, if we cannot help. We also appreciate open feedback on our cooperation.

Curiosity

We are fascinated by learning new things, be it's an innovative technology, a new business model, or a new approach to regulation.

Love for the work we do

We are real fans of our work! It is important for us to grow and develop professionally, to invest in development of the highest professional standards and strive to be reliable business partners and role models for future generations of lawyers.

Eagerness to share knowledge

We want to create a common space where anyone can benefit from the knowledge and experience of others. We believe that such space can be a source of support and inspiration for both: those who share, and those who seek for advice.
